Shrimp Scampi

A recipe to make a quick, garlicky, delicious shrimp scampi

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced shallots
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh chives
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 pounds large shrimp - peeled and deveined
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for high heat
  2. In a large bowl, mix together softened butter, olive olive garlic, shallots, chives, salt, pepper, and paprika. Add the shrimp and toss to coat
  3. Lightly oil grill grate. Cook the shrimp as close to the flame as possible for 2-3 minutes per side, or until opaque.
