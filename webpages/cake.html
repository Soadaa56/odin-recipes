Cake

Description

This is a recipe to make a delicious chocolate cake!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • .666 cup shortening
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Begin boiling 2 cups of water. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 8x13 inch pan. Sift together the flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, cocoa and salt. Set aside.
  2. Melt the shortening in the boiling water, then stir into the flour mixture until blended. Beat in the eggs, then stir in the vanilla.
  3. Pour batter into prepard pan. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
